Importance of the nutritional table

The labeling of edible products in Colombia is part of the current strategy for promoting public health.

Nutritional table provides consumers with clear and understandable nutritional information about a product, in a way that does not mislead or confuse, allowing them to make an informed choice and make decisions related to food that are beneficial to their health.

Main parts

  1. Serving size
  2. Amount of calories
  3. Percentage of daily value
  4. Nutrients that are recommended to be monitored
  5. Essential nutrients

    6. Important

    • Daily values ​​are based on a 2,000 calorie diet.
    • A better approximation would take into account your caloric requirement.
    • Calorie requirement varies by sex, and consistency of daily physical activity.
